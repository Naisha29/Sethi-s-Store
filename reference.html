Reference

Founded in 2008, CORE is the technology club at Delhi Public School, Dwarka. Their aim is to promote competence and excellence in the field of computer and information technology at school, as well as various inter school symposia. We have been providing our services in this school from past decades. Today these tiny toddlers whom we have known ever since then are now the esteemed members of CORE. One can check their website where you can find resource material made by these exceptionaly telented kids. These resources help newcomers navigate the fields of Web Development, Design, Competitive Programming, Quizzing, Crossword, 3D Design, Computational Linguistics, Game Development, Group Discussion, Machine Learning, Mobile App Development, Hardware, Cryptic Hunts, Motion Design, and Audio Editing.
